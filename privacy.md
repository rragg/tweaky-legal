## Introduction

Tweaky ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("Tweaky" or the "App").

By using Tweaky, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Photos and Images

- **What we collect:** Photos you choose to enhance using Tweaky
- **How we use it:** Photos are sent to our secure servers for AI enhancement processing
- **Storage:** Photos are cached temporarily for performance and **automatically deleted** from our servers within 30 days
- **We do NOT:** Store your photos permanently, share your photos with third parties, or use your photos for any purpose other than enhancement

### 2. Device Information

- **What we collect:** A randomly generated device identifier (UUID)
- **How we use it:** To track usage limits per device and manage subscriptions
- **Storage:** Stored locally on your device in secure iOS Keychain storage
- **We do NOT:** Collect your device's hardware identifiers, phone number, or IMEI

### 3. Usage Data

- **What we collect:** Number of images enhanced, timestamps of usage
- **How we use it:** To enforce subscription tier limits and improve our service
- **Storage:** Associated with your anonymous device identifier, not your personal identity

### 4. Subscription Information

- **What we collect:** Subscription status (free/premium), subscription type
- **How we use it:** To unlock premium features and manage your subscription
- **Storage:** Managed by Apple through App Store subscriptions
- **We do NOT:** Store your payment information - all payments are handled by Apple

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly **do not** collect:

- Your name, email, or contact information
- Your location data
- Your contacts or address book
- Your browsing history
- Data from other apps
- Advertising identifiers
- Any biometric data

---

## How We Protect Your Information

### Security Measures

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data is transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Secure Storage:** Device identifiers stored in iOS Keychain (hardware-encrypted)
- **Certificate Pinning:** Our app verifies server authenticity to prevent interception
- **Auto-Deletion:** Uploaded photos are automatically deleted after processing
- **No Third-Party Sharing:** We never share or sell your data

### Data Processing

- Photos are processed on secure Google Cloud Platform servers
- Processing typically completes within seconds
- Processed images are transmitted back to your device
- Original and processed images are deleted from servers within 30 days
